TNX (T-mobile Network eXperience) "How-To"

  1. Open Sprint account on GST (REMO).
  2. When prompted what you want to do within the account select Manage Account.
  3. Select Go Magenta from options
  4. Select TNX or T-Mobile Network Experience and verify eligibilty in NBA every time even if itâ€™s a bill pay/accessory purchase/upgrade/AAL/BYOD/etc.
  5. Once eligibility is verified, go back and tap Subscriber and scroll until you see Change SIM Card.
  6. Once you are on Change SIM Card, select T-Mobile Network and switch the SIM to the correct SIM card ZZZR060 (old SIMs) or ZZZR070 (new SIMs)
  7. Follow the process to zero out the magenta SIM and they have now been TNX'd.
